Akshansh Pareek

Researcher & Designer

Hi, I’m Akshansh Pareek — a researcher and designer working at the intersection of computational social science, NLP, participatory research, and algorithmic fairness. I currently collaborate with the University of Cambridge on research into institutional and geographic bias in academic publishing, and I’ve also worked with the MIT Media Lab on an empirical study examining positionality statements in fairness and transparency research. I earned my Bachelor of Design from Delhi Technological University (DTU), where my thesis explored regional and occupational bias in large language models.

Outside of research, I enjoy short 3D cinematography, lo‑fi art, yoga, chess, and basketball.
